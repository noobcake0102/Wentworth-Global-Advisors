Services

Operational leadership, on demand — sized to the problem.

Seven service lines, one operating standard. Each engagement is led personally by a senior executive operations advisor with quantified delivery accountability.

01 · Fractional & Interim Leadership

Fractional CEO, COO, Managing Director & VP Operations

Senior executive coverage for private equity portfolio companies and founder-led businesses that need a credible operating leader on Day One. Engagements span fractional COO services for $10M to $100M manufacturing companies, interim CEO for turnaround companies, and interim operations leader for private equity portfolio companies.

Typical: 6–12 months · 30–50 hours per week · retainer or hybrid.

02 · Operations Turnaround

Business Turnaround & Operational Recovery

For companies with poor on-time delivery, weak margins, customer escalations, or working capital trapped on the floor. We provide operational turnaround for underperforming companies, manufacturing backlog recovery strategy, and fix poor operational performance with a written, sequenced recovery plan and the leadership to execute it.

Typical: 9–12 months · 40–50 hours per week · hourly or hybrid.

03 · Post-M&A Integration

End-to-End Operations Integration After an Acquisition

Post merger integration consulting for small and mid-sized private equity buyers: headcount, IT, culture, product, and facility rationalization led as a single program. We are the operations leader for post-acquisition integration — the executive who turns the value-creation thesis into operational reality.

Typical: 3–9 months · 40 hours per week · project-based or retainer.

04 · Lean Six Sigma

Lean Six Sigma Implementation for Manufacturing

DMAIC-based diagnosis, SMED, TPM, SPC, balanced production, single-piece flow, and pull-system scheduling. We deliver lean six sigma implementation consulting grounded in floor-level execution — with the engineers, operators, and schedulers who will own it after we leave.

Typical: 6–12 months as part of broader operations leadership.

05 · Supply Chain Advisory

Strategic Supply Chain Optimization Consulting

End-to-end review of procurement, supplier qualification, stockroom design, kitting, MRP discipline, and supplier development. We fix supply chain inefficiencies, reduce material loss, and rebuild the supplier base for high-reliability and aerospace programs.

Typical: 1–3 month assessment · 30 hours per week · retainer.

06 · Operations Assessment

Operations Assessment & Improvement Services

A focused, finite engagement to diagnose root causes of underperformance, prioritize the fixes, and produce a written, sequenced recommendation. Often the first step before a full fractional COO services engagement — or a stand-alone deliverable for boards making decisions about the operations function.

Typical: 1–3 months · 30 hours per week · retainer.

07 · Executive Coaching

Coaching & Mentoring for New Operations Leaders

Structured one-on-one mentoring for first-time COOs, VPs of Operations, and Managing Directors stepping into a manufacturing operating role. Executive coaching for operations leaders grounded in the same toolkit we deploy ourselves — lean, Six Sigma, P&L, and customer recovery.

08 · Board Advisory

Non-Executive Director & Board Advisory

Independent board advisory services for operations: a credible operating voice in the boardroom for private-equity boards and founder-led companies. We bring the operating perspective the strategy and finance functions cannot.

What We Solve

The operating problems that keep ownership awake.

If any of the following describes your situation, we have likely solved it before.

  • Improve on-time delivery. Reset the production schedule, install pull-system flow, and rebuild customer confidence on past-due programs — the work of a credible consultant to fix late deliveries in manufacturing.
  • Reduce manufacturing costs. Material loss reduction, redesign for manufacturability, stockroom rationalization, and supplier consolidation — the toolkit of a senior operations improvement consultant.
  • Improve cash flow in manufacturing. Trapped WIP recovery, working capital reduction, and qualification of finished-good inventory for revenue conversion.
  • Fix supply chain inefficiencies. Procurement reset, supplier qualification, MRP discipline, and physical stockroom redesign.
  • Manufacturing backlog recovery strategy. Triage by program, redesign for producibility on the critical few, and customer-by-customer recovery commitments we will personally honor.
  • How to scale manufacturing operations. Move from one-good-batch to repeatable, validated, multi-machine, multi-part-number production at contract-rate volume — fractional COO services for $10M to $100M manufacturing companies ramping into committed contracts.
  • Operational turnaround for underperforming companies. Stabilize, diagnose, sequence, lead.
  • Operations leader for post-acquisition integration. Single point of accountability across headcount, IT, culture, product, and facility rationalization.
  • Business process redesign services. Rebuild the production, contract, configuration, and procurement processes that determine whether the operation can scale.
  • How to improve manufacturing margins. Pricing, productivity, scrap, throughput, and overhead absorption — all four levers, simultaneously, led by a high-reliability manufacturing consultant who has done the work before.
