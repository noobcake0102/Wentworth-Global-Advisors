About Wentworth

An executive operations advisor — not a slide-deck consultancy.

Wentworth Global Advisors was founded to give manufacturers, private equity sponsors, and boards a credible operating executive they can deploy on demand — for a turnaround, a critical vacancy, a post-merger integration, or a contract ramp where the cost of failure is unacceptable.

Founder & Principal

Nick Bobay — Operating CEO/COO/MD with 25+ years across aerospace, defense, satellite, and industrial manufacturing.

Nick Bobay is a career operating executive who has run businesses, led turnarounds, and integrated acquisitions across aerospace and defense electronics, LEO and MEO satellite hardware, high-reliability electronics, and industrial manufacturing. He has held the CEO, COO, Managing Director, and Vice President of Operations chair — not as a temporary visitor, but as the executive accountable for the P&L, the customer relationship, and the team.

His track record is built on three reinforcing strengths: a financial and strategic perspective shaped at the public-company level, a deep operational toolkit grounded in lean and Six Sigma, and a willingness to lead from the floor. Engineers know they can talk to him. Operators know he’ll roll up his sleeves. Customers know he will be the one who calls them back.

A career in plain English.

Nick’s career has been spent inside companies that make things — novel-process metallurgy components for aerospace and space vehicles; high-frequency, high-power tubes and solid-state RF systems for defense and satellite communications; ground stations for LEO and MEO satellite constellations; and a long catalogue of industrial design and manufacturing operations. Across those companies he has consistently been the executive owners and boards turn to when the operation needs to move from chaos to repeatable performance.

Today Wentworth Global Advisors offers that experience as a fractional and interim executive service. Engagements are sized to the problem — from a 30-day operations assessment to a multi-quarter integration — and led personally.

What clients can expect.

Within 90 days of a Wentworth engagement, most clients have a clear, quantitative diagnosis of what is wrong, a written plan with priority and sequencing, the leadership team aligned behind it, and the first measurable improvement on the board. Within nine months, the typical operation is favorable on margin, on schedule with customers, and run by the existing team without us in the room.

We are not the right fit for every situation. We do not offer M&A due diligence on its own (we participate as part of broader operational assessments), we do not lead ERP or MRP coded improvements, and we do not deploy IT systems beyond program-management oversight. When that is what you need, we’ll say so.

Credentials & toolkit.

Lean Six Sigma methodologies (DMAIC, SMED, TPM, SPC). Integrated Product Development (IPD) for design-for-manufacturability and design-for-testability. Pull-system scheduling and balanced production. Environmental qualification (MIL-STD-810 and commercial adaptations). Stockroom redesign and inventory rationalization. Configuration control and contract specification cleanup. P&L, working-capital, and cash-flow management at division and enterprise scale. Direct customer recovery on past-due programs.

Where we’re headed.

Wentworth Global Advisors is a deliberately senior firm. The long-term vision is a small, hand-picked bench of operating executives who can be deployed on the same standard of leadership Nick personally delivers today — international operational leadership for companies that need critical direction leading to world-class performance.
